Here are my typography designs showcasing the 8 different typographic systems, creating these designs were apart of my module to help me break free from traditonal horizontal and vertical grid systems. I had to research each typographic system and use Adobe illustrator to produce one layout for each system to be placed in my website. In this part of the module I had specific requirements that I had to apply to my designs, for instance I had to choose between 4 typefaces and was constricted in a 200mm x 200mm canvas size, as well this I could only use one colour in addition to black and white and was allowed to use shapes or lines however, no images.
